About

Oliver Pryce

Founder, Meshless

I’m building Meshless so anyone can turn an idea into a precise, printable 3D model and, eventually, a manufactured object — without years of CAD training.

My background is shipping real products: I founded my first startup at 16 around voice AI for medical practices, built the software myself, and learned sales by walking into clinics and demoing until it worked.

For the last year and a half I’ve been deep in ML for 3D geometry — hundreds of experiments to understand where frontier models break on real CAD — and Meshless is the prototype that comes out of that work.
